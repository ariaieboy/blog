From 8 of November 2026 We don't have any access to the internet in my country. In this post I don't want to talk about the politics or why this happened and who is right and who is wrong.

It's not a technical post either. It's just a story that we are the main characters in 2026.

Everything started from the Thursday night. After work when I arrived at my home. I found out that my internet connection offline. Not only the internet shutdown but also all the local websites inaccessible.

For the first hours I thought it's like other times that they limit the internet access, and the access returns after a few days. But this time it different.

For years the government and the business try to make their apps and websites to work even without the internet. 

All websites moved their server inside the country and added OTP login using SMS so if we lost internet connection for hours or days like previous blackouts users could access to their sites and apps.

We also have lots of local services for example we have more than 7 local messenger app replacing telegram and WhatsApp those apps exists so when the internet access is limited people can use those apps to communicate with each-other. 

This time not only the internet is shutdown but also all the communication channels is blocked. All website including the government website is offline. And you cannot send or receive AN SMS.

And for the calls you only can call your loved ones in 8AM to 8PM~10PM after that when the night comes you are all alone. You can't text you can't call.

For sure, they can't block all communications for a long period.

** It's NOT NORTH KOREA

When you talk to outsiders they compare IRAN with NORTH KOREA. But it's not comparable.

In Iran, we have technology, and the people life depends on it. If we didn't have internet from the first place, and the people was brainwashed that there nothing exists outside the IRAN. It was easier to live without the internet and the modern communication technologies.

So please when you read an article about IRAN internet blackout DO NOT COMPARE IT WITH NORTH KOREA. 

I think in Saturday some government websites, and some official news agencies started to work and after it apps and websites that their server is inside the country started to get online.

** Online But not Working

As I said before the SMS is blocked in the country you cannot log in to more than 95% of the websites. The official and government websites the login system only works with SMS OTP and in other websites it's AN SMS OTP or Google login. And as you can tell Google is not accessible here.

** No Google, Hello to 2026 Website URL List

If you are old enough you might remember the telephone books that people write their loved ones number in that telephone book.

I saw lots off people searching for official website URL they all used Google to find the website they want since the Google is not available. People start to create website URL list, so they can access banks or internal websites by entering the website URL instead of searching in Google. 

** SMS is Back But Partially

After 2-3 days OTP SMS for official websites is back and after around 5-6 days other website can send OTP code only if their message pattern don't contain any English letter you only can send Persian letters and numbers. And every pattern must get accepted by the officials.

** Google is Online not anything else

After about 7 days people can access Google in some regions. It's obvious in the picture above when the Google access is restored in some areas my website stats shows improvement. But that's not mean we have access to the internet. 

You can't call it INTERNET. 

** It's FilterNET+ 

We don't call this internet. Before the blackout we had FilterNET. It's internet but lots of social media apps and websites are filtered and censored by the government. And in the other hand lots of global services strict Iranian access to their platforms because of the sanctions.

For example, we don't have access to YouTube or Telegram because of the government filters and Amazon and Amazon cloud and Google Cloud services because of the sanctions. 

Because of those we call it FilterNET now it's FilterNET+. We don't access to a lot of websites that we had access before the blackout for example Cloudflare is not accessible while before the blackout we had access to it.

** NoBody Can Stop US

As I said we are sanctioned from the outside and filtered from the inside but NoBody can stop us. Our people adopt quickly.

For example in the case of Telegram While it's filtered by Government but about 50M out of 80M people use it using proxies.

And that's why this time they blocked all the communication channels. So nobody can share their way of connection to others. 

** 15 days passed another 15 days is on the way

It's been 15 days that the FilterNET that we used before is offline, and we only have access to a handful of necessary apps and websites. Google,Gmail in some hours and GitHub is accessable for about 24 hours.We don't know its temprorary or permanent.
